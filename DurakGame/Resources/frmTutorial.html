Tutorial

Durak has no winners. Each player is dealt a certain number of cards based on the deck size. There are attackers and defenders.

Attackers must look at the card on the playing field and see if they have a card in their hand which is of the same face value but higher number and play that card on the field.

The person on the right of the attacker is the defender, the defender looks at the top card on the playing field and must put down a card of the same suit but higher value or a trump card. If the defender cannot put down a card then they must pick up all the cards on the playing field and put it into their hand.

If the defender was successful in stopping all of the attacks then the defender becomes the new attacker, otherwise the person to the right of the defender is the new attacker.
